Common Questions

1. What services do you offer?

We specialize in pressure washing, polymeric sand application, and surface sealing for residential and commercial properties. Our services include cleaning and maintaining sidewalks, pool decks, roofs, and other exterior areas.

2. How do I get a quote?

You can easily request a quote by filling out our contact form, giving us a call, or sending us an email. Provide as much detail as possible, and we’ll get back to you with a personalized estimate.

3. What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Jacksonville, FL, and the surrounding areas. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just ask!

4. How do I prepare my property for service?

Before we arrive, please remove any obstacles, such as furniture or vehicles, from the area to be serviced. We’ll provide specific instructions based on the service you’ve requested.

5. Is your work guaranteed?

Yes, we stand by the quality of our work. If you’re not completely satisfied, please let us know, and we’ll do everything we can to make it right.
